## Projects

**NestInsights — Consumer Intelligence Platform** &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live](https://github.com/zain-ul-abideen-1/Nestinsights-Consumer-Intelligence-Platform)

A production-grade review analytics platform. Scrapes, classifies, and extracts insights from customer feedback using a custom ML pipeline (TF-IDF + LinearSVC, 89% accuracy on 10,500+ samples). Integrates BERT and BiLSTM for sentiment analysis, fake review detection, and aspect-level extraction. Features a Business Intelligence Hub with regression-based revenue forecasting, competitor analysis, AI-generated executive reports, and a Groq-powered real-time AI assistant.

**Product Review Classification — NLP System**

End-to-end NLP pipeline classifying product reviews by sentiment and category. Built text preprocessing, tokenization, TF-IDF and embedding vectorization, and evaluated multiple ML/DL models reporting accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 across each.

---

**Vehicle Detection in Challenging Conditions — Deep Learning**

YOLO and Fast R-CNN approach for vehicle detection under low-visibility and adverse imaging conditions, with model optimization to maximize accuracy under real-world constraints.

---

**Payment App UI — Android**

Full Figma prototype for a modern Android payment application, designed with clean visual hierarchy, accessible UI patterns, and a user-first flow.

---

**Smart Home Automation — Cisco Packet Tracer**

Simulated smart home network with router, switch, home gateway, and wireless IoT devices, featuring centralized monitoring and automated device control.
